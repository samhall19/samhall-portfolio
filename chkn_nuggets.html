CHKN NUGGETS

Game name: CHKN NUGGETS

Platform: PC / VR

My Role: Minigame Designer

Tools Used: Unity, Confluence, Jira

Overview

CHKN NUGGETS is a fast-paced, competitive party game where players build wacky, animal-based characters and battle across a series of chaotic minigames. The game emphasizes humor, unpredictability, and fun, with each session offering fresh challenges and surprises.

My Contributions

  • Minigame Design: Concepted, prototyped, and iterated multiple minigames to ensure they were engaging and fun for all players.
  • Gameplay Mechanics: Designed the character building system and ability interactions to create a chaotic but readable experience.
  • Level & Scene Setup: Built modular minigame environments, balancing pacing and player engagement across sessions.
  • Playtesting & Iteration: Conducted extensive playtests, gathered player feedback, and refined mechanics for fun and replayability.
  • Collaboration: Worked with art, programming, and design teams to ensure a polished, cohesive experience.
