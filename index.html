About Me

I studied game design at Falmouth University from 2016–2019, where I built a portfolio of small games. I later completed a master's degree while developing my first commercial title, International Space Banana. After its release, I joined Coatsink as a junior designer and quickly moved into a mid-level role, taking on lead responsibilities for a smaller project.

I have extensive experience designing for VR, as well as PC and mobile platforms. I consider myself a technical designer — skilled in coding, visual scripting, and technical production workflows. I’m an expert Unity developer and familiar with Unreal Engine.
