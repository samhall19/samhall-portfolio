About Me

For the last 5 years I have worked at Coatsink software as a game designer, where I have gained extensive experience developing for VR, PC and console. I have taken large commercial projects from concept to release, and am skilled in general game design and level design. Alongside this I am skilled in technical aspects of development, including coding, visual scripting, and using all the tools within a game engine. I studied game design at Falmouth University from 2016–2020, where I built a portfolio of small games, and achieved a masters degree.
