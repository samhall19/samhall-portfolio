International Space Banana

Game name: International Space Banana

Platform: PC

My Role: C# Programmer, Gameplay & Puzzle Designer

Tools Used: Unity

Overview

International Space Banana is my first commercially released game, a two-person startup project. Players control a banana floating in low gravity with intentionally difficult-to-control mechanics. The game combines humor, philosophy, and puzzle-solving, progressively increasing complexity as players navigate its comical, low-gravity world.

My Contributions

C# Programming

Implemented core gameplay mechanics and physics for unique low-gravity controls.

Gameplay & Puzzle Design

Designed puzzles that evolve upon the core mechanics and challenge the player in creative ways.

Level Design

Crafted environments and whitebox-to-finished stages to maximize engagement.

Voice & Narrative Integration

Integrated story, voiceover, and humor into gameplay mechanics.
